About Me

Hello! I'm a current sophomore at Tufts University pursuing bachelor degrees in Computer Science and Cognitive Brain Science. At present, I'm conducting research at UMass Lowell on Human Computer Interaction with a focus on security. I'm also working with SheHacksBoston 2019, an all-female and non-binary hackathon, as their Logistics Chair. At Tufts, I'm a member of the Women in Computer Science club and the Campus Section Editor and a writer for the Observer Magazine.
