About Me

Anita Lam is a current Sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Brain Science. At present, Anita is conducting research at UMass Lowell on Human Computer Interaction with a focus on security. She is also working with SheHacksBoston 2019, an all-female and non-binary hackathon, as Logistics Chair. At Tufts, Anita is a member of the Women in Computer Science club and the Campus Section Editor and writer for the Observer Magazine.
